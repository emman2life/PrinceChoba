Prince Choba Campsite

Prince Choba Campsite is a part of land on the coast of E.Africa Tanzania just touching the shores of Indian ocean. It is located just outside the small town of Pangani and it is almost 34km on the south of Tanga region and 33km East of Muheza district. The name Choba came from the small village which is about 1.5km from the Campsite The name Chop have its root and history around the locals who are living in the village. Prince Choba has its own soul as its bring people to their relaxing moods and creates a memorable experience that keep them coming back.

Our houses

At Prince Choba we have Beach Bungalows that are constructed with local materials such as wild woods and coconut palms just like the locals living along the shores of indian ocean and away from all modern constructed houses. Enjoy the coastal life, feel the vibration of waves of the sea right under your pillows, relax and listen to the sound of the ocean from the comfort of your bungalow. Houses are equipped with solar power so never run out of power for your devices.
